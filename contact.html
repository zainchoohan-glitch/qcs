Frequently Asked Questions

Is Qr Code Scanner really free?

Yes! All features of Qr Code Scanner are completely free with no hidden costs, subscriptions, or premium tiers. We believe QR code tools should be accessible to everyone.

Do you store my QR code data?

No. All QR code scanning and generation happens entirely in your browser. Your data never leaves your device, and we don't have access to any QR codes you scan or generate.

Can I use Qr Code Scanner for commercial purposes?

Yes! You can use Qr Code Scanner for both personal and commercial purposes free of charge. However, please review our Terms & Conditions for details.

Which browsers are supported?

Qr Code Scanner works on all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, and mobile browsers. For camera scanning, your browser needs to support the MediaDevices API.

How do I report a bug or request a feature?

You can use the contact form above or email us directly at bugs@scanqrpro.com for bug reports or feedback@scanqrpro.com for feature requests. We value your input!

Do you have a mobile app?

Qr Code Scanner is a web application that works perfectly on mobile browsers. You can add it to your home screen for quick access. Native mobile apps may be available in the future.
