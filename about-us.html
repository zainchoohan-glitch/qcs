About Us

We're on a mission to make QR code technology accessible, secure, and easy to use for everyone.

Our Story

ScanQR Pro was born from a simple observation: QR codes have become an integral part of our daily lives, yet many existing QR code tools compromise user privacy by sending data to remote servers or bombarding users with advertisements.

We believe that scanning and generating QR codes should be fast, secure, and completely private. That's why we built ScanQR Pro with a client-side-first approach – all processing happens right in your browser, ensuring your data never leaves your device.

Today, thousands of users worldwide trust ScanQR Pro for their QR code needs, from sharing WiFi passwords to creating custom QR codes for their businesses.

Our Technology

Client-Side Processing

All QR code scanning and generation happens directly in your web browser using JavaScript. This means faster processing, enhanced privacy, and no dependency on external servers.

Modern Web Standards

Built with HTML5, CSS3, and vanilla JavaScript, ScanQR Pro leverages cutting-edge web technologies like the MediaDevices API for camera access and Canvas API for QR code generation.

Mobile-First Design

Responsive design ensures ScanQR Pro works seamlessly across all devices – from smartphones and tablets to desktop computers.

Security by Design

We implement security best practices including suspicious URL detection, HTTPS-ready deployment, and no third-party tracking or analytics that could compromise your privacy.
