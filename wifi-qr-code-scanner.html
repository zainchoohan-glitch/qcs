Print & Display

Print the QR code and place it where guests can easily scan it

💡 WiFi QR Code Tips

  • Print the QR code on high-quality paper for best scanning results
  • Place the QR code in a visible location (e.g., near entrance, on fridge)
  • Laminate printed QR codes to protect from wear and tear
  • Test the QR code with your own device before sharing
  • Generate a new QR code whenever you change your WiFi password
  • Keep a digital backup of your WiFi QR code

🔒 Privacy Notice

Your WiFi credentials are processed entirely in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server. Be careful where you share or display your WiFi QR code, as anyone with access can connect to your network.
