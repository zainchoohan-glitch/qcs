QR codes can contain malicious links. Always verify the URL before visiting:

  • Check for suspicious shortened URLs (bit.ly, tinyurl, etc.)
  • Verify the domain matches the expected website
  • Be cautious of QR codes in public places
  • Never scan QR codes that ask for sensitive information
  • Use antivirus software on your device
